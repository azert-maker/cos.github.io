The Concept

Simple.
Brutal.
Addictive.

CONFESS OR SIP is the podcast where silence is not an option. Four guests. One host. Five rounds of increasingly personal, uncomfortable, and revealing questions.

The rule is deceptively simple: answer truthfully — or drink. No half-answers. No deflections. The host decides if you're dodging. The glass is always watching.

As the rounds progress, the questions escalate. What starts as light banter turns into confessions nobody saw coming.

The Show

How CONFESS OR SIP
Works.

CONFESS OR SIP was born from one simple observation: people are only truly honest when they have something to lose.

We created a format where the cost of silence is a drink, and the cost of lying is two drinks. Suddenly everyone has a price.

Four guests — friends, strangers, colleagues, exes — sit around a table with their drinks and a host who has zero mercy. The questions are written by our team and curated from fan submissions.

Nobody comes out the same.

Episode Structure
Round 1 → Warm-up (easy questions)
Round 2 → Personal territory begins
Round 3 → Comfort zone: obliterated
Round 4 → Confessions nobody expected
Round 5 → Fan questions — the wildcard
Guest Format
1 fixed host + 3 rotating guests per episode. Possible combinations: best friends, couples, strangers, rivals, celebrities, creators, athletes. Each season has a finale "Champions Episode".

About

Born From

CONFESS OR SIP started as a simple challenge between friends: what if you had to answer? No phone. No lawyer. No "pass".

We tested it at a party. By round three, someone was crying happy tears and two people admitted things they'd kept secret for years. We knew we had something.

The podcast is the party. We just invite you to watch — and submit your questions for round five.
